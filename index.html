Testimonials

About us

Woodpeckers Summer Camp is a Romania-based project meant to bring together high schoolers from all around the world. We await the application of more than 150 students willing to make unforgettable memories in the heart of nature. The program takes place from July 17th to July 30th. You can apply at the provided e-mail address until the 30th of June.
